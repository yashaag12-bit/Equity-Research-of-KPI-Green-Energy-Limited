This project presents a comprehensive equity research and valuation analysis of KPI Green Energy Ltd., focusing on financial performance, industry positioning, growth prospects, and intrinsic valuation.
The objective was to evaluate the company from an investment perspective using structured financial analysis and valuation techniques similar to those used in investment banking and equity research roles.
**Key Objectives**
Conduct in-depth financial statement analysis
Assess business model, industry outlook, and growth drivers
Perform valuation using DCF and relative valuation methods
Identify key risks, opportunities, and investment rationale
**Key Insights**
Identified major growth drivers and expansion opportunities
Evaluated financial strength and capital efficiency
Estimated intrinsic valuation and investment attractiveness
Highlighted key risks impacting future performance
